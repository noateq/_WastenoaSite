Adsorption, absorption, thermal and catalytic processes are widely used for gas purification including vapor and gas impurities. Russky Katalizator JSC in association with leading Russian chemical technology universities has developed innovative gas purification on ceramic foam filters technology. This technology is based on adsorption and catalytic oxidation of gas flow and is used by several Russian industrial plants. This gas purification technology almost entirely (by 99%) reduces harmful substance emission into the atmosphere and significantly abates operational costs as compared to other purification technologies e. g. thermal process using natural gas burners.

Stainless steel sectional body with 2 mm wall thickness supports easy catalyst regeneration and, if needed, replacement. The catalyst is regenerated by water washing under pressure or burning in the oven at a temperature of 400-450 °C. Regeneration frequency depends on gas flow pollution degree and is in average one time per 3 months.

Catalyst regeneration becomes more easy and efficient with additional maintenance service contract. We perform maintenance service within the short period of time (within 3 days excluding shipping) even if you are located in other city.

Catalyst supported on ceramic foam is much more efficient than honeycomb monolith catalyst. A gas particles sorption takes place in the whole volume of the catalyst while honeycomb catalyst efficiency abates because of inlets clogging.
